Aluminium Fabricator

Published Date : 16/10/2018
Closing Date : 15/02/2019
Company ALUSIL PRODUCTION LTEE
Economic Sector Manufacturing-Metal products
State / Province Camp Fouquereaux Phoenix
Country Mauritius
Duties of Job

- Cutting and processing of aluminium to assemble framing for windows and doors based on various job specifications.

- Glazing of aluminium doors and windows

- Knowledge and experience for the use of power tools and drills are a must.

- Assembly of window and door frames

- Able to read plans and drawings
Job Summary

Full time positions are currently available within our Alusil workshop for candidates who has previous experience in the assembly of aluminium windows, doors or curtain walls. This person must be highly motivated and be able to work under pressure.
Other Skills / Competencies

- Good and positive attitude with the ability to work as part of a team and autonomously.

- Good communication skills.

- A keen eye for detail to produce a quality product.

- Ability to perform physical and sometimes repetitive work.

- Positive work ethics.

- Reliable and punctual.
Academic Qualification Required FORM 3
Experience Required 1 year
Salary Proposed 5001 - 10000
Currency Type Mauritian Rupees
Working Hours 8 am - 5 pm
